What tier am I in
To find out your tier - please visit https://www.gov.uk/find-coronavirus-local-restrictions and type in your postcode 
Why a tiered system
to ensure places that have increased signs of outbreak are managed and the virus is suppressed and the R is kept below 1.

What tiers are my family in? 
To find out your family and friends tier - please visit https://www.gov.uk/find-coronavirus-local-restrictions and type in their postcode. Alternately you can visit - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/full-list-of-local-restriction-tiers-by-area - to find a full list of the areas and what tier they fall under.

Can I travel to another country 
if you are in a tier 4 area, then you should not travel aboard. You will need a legally permitted reason to leave home. A full checklist of what do to before, during and after travel can be found here - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/travel-advice-novel-coronavirus. You also need to check the country you are travelling to for their advice on quarantine on arrival.

You will receive statutory sick pay for the time you are off work and may also be eligible for test and trace support payment. Full details of the finical support available can be found here - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/coronavirus-covid-19-what-to-do-if-youre-employed-and-cannot-work
